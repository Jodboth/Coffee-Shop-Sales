**Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (SQL Project)**

This project analyzes transactional sales data from a coffee shop to 
uncover key business insights across stores, products, time, and customer demand patterns.

**Key Analysis Performed**

**Total Sales Performance**
Sales calculated per store, product category, and product type.
Revenue trends identified across locations.

**Store Performance**
Stores ranked by total revenue and transaction volume.
Hell’s Kitchen identified as the top-performing branch.

**Product Analysis**
Coffee, tea, bakery items, and drinking chocolate are the top-selling products.
Low-performing products categorized using sales ranges (High, Medium, Low).

**Time-Based Trends**
Peak sales hours occur between 7 AM – 10 AM.
Friday, Thursday, and Monday are the busiest days.

**Location Insights**
Sales distribution varies across store locations.
Product performance differs by branch based on customer demand.

**Monthly Performance**
June recorded the highest sales activity.

**Tools & Techniques**
SQL (GROUP BY, CTEs, CASE, WINDOW FUNCTIONS)
Aggregation and time-based analysis
Ranking and performance segmentation
